the x-path //h2[@class='nameclass...'] will search from top. To search from current node. you can use .(dot) like .//h2[@class='nameclass...'].

for card in cards:
    name = card.find_element_by_xpath(".//h2[@class='nameclass...']")
    print(name.text)
